About Stewart Datawright Labs

Our Mission

At Stewart Datawright Labs, we believe that well-crafted software is a form of modern craftsmanship. Our mission is to build digital tools that are not only powerful and efficient but also elegant and intuitive. Whether we're architecting a complex data system or designing a simple mobile utility, we are driven by a passion for quality, precision, and solving real-world problems through technology.

The Founder

Robert Stewart

Founder & Lead Developer

Robert is a technologist with a Master's degree in Computer Science Software Engineering and extensive professional experience as an IT Applications Administrator. With deep expertise in C#, SQL, and SSIS, he has spent years architecting and engineering complex ETL solutions and back-office tools.

His passion for technology extends beyond his professional life into PC building, smart home automation, and gaming. Stewart Datawright Labs was founded to merge this lifelong passion with his professional expertise, creating a studio dedicated to building high-quality software for all platforms.
