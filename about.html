About Stewart Datawright Labs

Founded by Robert Stewart, Stewart Datawright Labs is dedicated to crafting reliable, maintainable software and data systems. Our goal: to transform raw data into actionable insight, and ideas into working applications.

Robert Stewart — Founder & Data Engineer

Robert has over a decade of experience working with data integration, ETL processes, and custom application development. His background in C#, SQL, and SSIS has helped companies streamline workflows, improve data accuracy, and unlock new possibilities for their operations.

Beyond technical skill, Robert believes in clear communication, thorough documentation, and creating solutions that stand the test of time. Whether building desktop utilities, mobile add-ons, or complete data platforms, he approaches each project with a craftsman’s mindset.
